Current Time:
Welcome to my web app, named Climate Defiant. Let's begin by running through a user story of the purpose of this app.

Imagine you are running late and don't have time to go and check the weather, or you are just so out of it that you can't think and make sense of what the weather conditions mean. Now, you want to know exactly what you need without putting any thought into it (because we're big on making life easier these days). Now you can just open the app from your bookmarks on your home page, or (in the future the app) and see exactly what you will need clearly shown through an image. Along with the image is a sentence also reflecting what you should take.
Now for those with a bit more time, you can scroll down to see what the conditions actually are right now. A cool little addition.
There is also a section aptly titled "Today sounds like". This section is supposed to reflect the mood of the weather through music, so for those who want some tunes to get ready to in the morning, or who are unable to see the image then they can listen to the mood of the weather.
